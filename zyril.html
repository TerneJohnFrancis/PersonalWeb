    Name: Zyril Magnus Satory


      Role: Assault Juggernaut/ Heavy Support Trooper


      Rank: Master Sergeant


      Callsign: Lima-03


      Status: Alive


      Backstory:

      • Early Life

        • Zyril Magnus Satory was an outgoing child until his parents died in the line of duty in the remote Siberian Tundra. As a result, he was raised by his aunt Cortana, and so lived in the same house with the four for six years.

      • Career

        • As time progressed, Zyril was also continually being trained by his uncle August as a Heavy Support Trooper, specializing in Heavy Assault tactics and Anti-Vehicle Support.

      • Battle for Somalia

        • During the ensuing firefight after the flare gun misfire, Zyril, having learned from Schwefel, immediately fired up his M134 Minigun and mowed down every enemy in front of him.
